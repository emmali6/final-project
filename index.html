In the United States, there are 424 National Park Units, and 63 of those have the "national park" title. Some span multiple states and some are only located in one state. In this website, you can learn about each park and what it has to offer. You'll be able to filter by region and then by state. If you've already been to some, you can fill out the survey to see which parks are most visited!
